Social Exchange Institute is a women-led company with over 40 years of combined experience focused on producing social justice centered media campaigns and content, providing capacity building and training to diverse audiences, and raising awareness about today's social, economic and health disparities.

Mission

To create social awareness and transformative change for those most affected by social inequities through the use of innovative media and collaborative research.

Vision

Social Exchange Institute envisions a society that continuously and actively uses social change practices that strengthens the “collective us” for sustained equity. Acknowledging that listening and dialogue should exist to raise social consciousness and to shift the narrative and norms around topics of abuse, oppression and trauma.
